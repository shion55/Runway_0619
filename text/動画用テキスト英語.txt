My first love
was apparently a girl
named Chi-chan
It's just what my parents say
I have no memory of it

In elementary and middle school
I made myself believe I liked
a girl in my class
I even thought
I truly did

The summer of eighth grade
a boy my age
I met at camp
called me by my first name
when everyone else only used my last
I wanted to remember it forever
Maybe that was...

Seeing him again after nine years
he was already wearing a ring
"Do you still go to church, Shion?"
he asked in an unfamiliar voice

Whenever I hear the label for what I am
my heart rate spikes
Hoping the subject will quickly pass
I hold my breath for a moment

Since turning twenty
I smile bitterly more often
Everyone can see me
and yet
they don't see me at all
I think this all the time

Since I'll be alone anyway
I want to live in a way
that at least I can be proud of!
